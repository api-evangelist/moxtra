Internal audit teams are increasingly adopting AI to handle repetitive tasks like evidence review, anomaly detection, and continuous monitoring, but only when humans remain accountable for final decisions. The key to defensible AI-assisted audits is clear workflow design with human review checkpoints, complete audit trails, and explicit documentation of how conclusions were reached.
